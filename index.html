About Us

Guangzhou Chiguoguo Fruit Co., Ltd. is a professional fruit import and export company, committed to providing high-quality fresh fruits to global customers. We adhere to the core values of 'quality assurance, timely response, and responsible after-sales service', winning trust and praise from domestic and international customers with our professional team and strict quality control.

As an industry leader, we not only provide diversified fruit products but also offer comprehensive service support to our customers, including customized procurement solutions, international logistics solutions, and after-sales tracking services, ensuring that every customer gets the best cooperation experience.

产品展示

Select high-quality grape varieties from various places, including Red Globe grapes, Thompson Seedless grapes, Black Queen grapes, seedless grapes, etc., which have a sweet and juicy taste.

Citrus fruits such as tangerines and navel oranges are juicy and sweet, and are rich in vitamins.

There are many kinds of high-quality Hami melons, such as Bamliu Wang and Xizhoumi. They are sweet and delicious, and rich in nutrients.
